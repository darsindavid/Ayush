Ayurveda

Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

Diseases Treated: Stress, anxiety, fatigue, adrenal insufficiency, insomnia.

Anti-Aging Effects: Ashwagandha lowers oxidative stress and inflammation, which influences biological aging. It also improves cognitive function and normalizes energy levels.

Biomarkers: Lowers cortisol levels (the stress hormone) and inflammatory cytokines like TNF-alpha and IL-6.

Tulsi (Ocimum sanctum)

Diseases Treated: Respiratory infections, stress, inflammation, digestion.

Anti-Aging Effects: Antioxidants in Tulsi help fight oxidative stress and inflammation responsible for aging.

Biomarkers: Reduces oxidative stress biomarkers like malondialdehyde and inflammation biomarkers like CRP and IL-6.

Neem (Azadirachta indica)

Diseases Treated: Acne, eczema, diabetes, bacterial, and fungal infections.

Anti-Aging Effects: The antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties of neem provide protection against premature skin aging and support healthy aging.

Biomarkers: Decreases markers of oxidative stress and inflammatory cytokines while potentially improving collagen levels, which are markers for skin health.

Turmeric (Curcuma longa)

Diseases Treated: Arthritis, digestive disorders, cardiovascular diseases, cancer.

Anti-Aging Effects: Turmeric contains a bioactive element called curcumin that helps to reduce DNA damage due to its potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Biomarkers: Lowers levels of oxidative stress markers, such as 8-OHdG, and inflammatory markers like IL-1 and IL-6.

Yoga & Naturopathy Plants

Ginger (Zingiber officinale)

Diseases to be Treated: Nausea, indigestion, arthritis, and respiration problems.

Anti-Aging Effects: Antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties reduce cellular damage and inflammation.

Biomarkers: Reduces markers of oxidative stress, like malondialdehyde, and inflammation, such as CRP.

Coriander (Coriandrum sativum)

Diseases to be Treated: Digestive disorders, high cholesterol, and infections.

Anti-Aging Effects: Antioxidant action promotes healthy cell protection and maintains good health.

Biomarkers: Reduces markers of oxidative stress, favoring cholesterol levels.

Fennel (Foeniculum vulgare)

Diseases to be Treated: Digestive issues, respiratory problems, and inflammation.

Anti-Aging Effects: Antioxidant properties help combat oxidative stress.

Biomarkers: Reduces oxidative stress markers and supports digestive health.

Lavender (Lavandula angustifolia)

Diseases Treated: Anxiety, insomnia, and depression.

Anti-Aging Effects: Relaxation effects reduce stress and improve sleep, indirectly supporting healthy aging.

Biomarkers: Reduces cortisol levels and promotes better sleep quality.

Unani

Senna (Senna alexandrina)

Diseases Treated: Constipation, digestive disorders.

Anti-Aging Effects: Senna promotes normal bowel movement, which is an integral part of detoxification and overall health.

Biomarkers: Promotes digestive enzyme activity and reduces biomarkers of constipation.

Psyllium (Plantago ovata)

Diseases Treated: Constipation, cholesterol management, blood sugar control.

Anti-Aging Effects: Psyllium helps with its fiber content in digestion and the regulation of bowels, hence contributing to overall health.

Biomarkers: Improves cholesterol and blood sugar markers.

Hibiscus (Hibiscus sabdariffa)

Diseases Treated: Hypertension, digestive disorders, infections.

Anti-Aging Effects: Antioxidant properties of hibiscus help in reducing oxidative stress and support cardiovascular health.

Biomarkers: Blood pressure reduction and reduction in markers of oxidative stress.

Liquorice (Glycyrrhiza glabra)

Diseases Treated: Digestive problems, respiratory conditions, adrenal fatigue.

Anti-Aging Effects: The anti-inflammatory, soothing action of liquorice contributes to general health and skin vitality.

Biomarkers: Inflammation markers are reduced, while the adrenals are supported.

Siddha

Andrographis (Andrographis paniculata)

Diseases Treated: Respiratory infections, immune disorders, inflammation.

Anti-Aging Effects: Andrographis improves immune function and reduces inflammation and may have a positive impact on overall health.

Biomarkers: Increases immune markers, such as lymphocyte count, and reduces inflammatory cytokines.

Arjuna (Terminalia arjuna)

Diseases Treated: Heart disease, hypertension, cholesterol problems.

Anti-Aging Effects: Arjuna maintains cardiovascular health and diminishes oxidative stress, which is an essential factor in healthy aging.

Biomarkers: Reduces blood pressure and cholesterol levels; diminishes markers of oxidative stress.

Kadukkai (Terminalia chebula)

Diseases Treated: Digestive disorders, detoxification, metabolic disorders.

Anti-Aging Effects: Kadukkai aids in detoxification and digestion and thus generally leads to vitality.

Biomarkers: Improvement in digestive enzymatic activity, reduction in oxidative stress markers.

Pippali (Piper longum)

Diseases Treated: Respiratory disorders, digestive disorders.

Anti-Aging Effects: Improved digestion and hence better absorption aids in the general state of health and assimilation of various nutrients.

Biomarkers: Enhances the bioavailability of other nutrients as well as reduces oxidative stress.

Homeopathy

Echinacea (Echinacea purpurea)

Diseases Treated: Immune system, respiratory infections.

Anti-Aging Effects: Improvement in immune competency and reduction of infection load to enhance overall health.

Biomarkers: Increases white blood cell count—immune markers and lowers inflammatory cytokines level.

Aconite (Aconitum napellus)

Diseases Treated: Acute stress, fever, anxiety.

Anti-Aging Effects: Relieves acute stress and anxiety to help promote general health and well-being.

Biomarkers: Lowers biomarkers of stress through supporting adrenal function.

Belladonna (Atropa belladonna)

Diseases Treated: High fever, inflammation, spasm.

Anti-Aging Effects: Lowers acute inflammation and fever, which may have a positive impact on overall health.

Biomarkers: Biomarkers of inflammation and biomarkers associated with fever can be lowered.

Arnica (Arnica montana)

Diseases Treated: Bruises, sprains, muscle soreness.

Anti-Aging Effects: Reduces inflammation and thus promotes healing, helping to keep the body functioning and vital.

Biomarkers: Inflammation markers are reduced and tissue repair is enhanced.
